Basic Troop Information

Our Troop Meets at Garden Street United Methodist Church in Bellingham, Washington. We meet Thursdays at 7pm in the basement.

Check the Upcoming Events by going to the Calender for an idea of what we are doing this month.

Core Values

Here at Troop 4020 we have some Core Values that we pride our selves in. These Core Valuse are:
