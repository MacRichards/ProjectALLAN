About Troop 4020

Troop 20 (Officially 4020)

Boy Led, Patrol Driven!

Bellingham, Washington

Founded in 1921, 95 years old!

Scoutmaster: Adam Niemur Sponsor

Organization: Garden Street United Methodist Men's Fellowship

We average about 30 scouts, year after year!

There's room for you!!!

Our Core Values In addition to the Eight Methods of Scouting , our Scouts and Adult Leaders have adopted eight "Core Values" that direct the attitude and actions of Troop 4020's program. These Core Values include:

  • Being Welcoming - to every person wanting to participate.
  • Being Boy Led - an outgrowth of leadership development and adult mentor-ship.
  • Offering a Balanced Program - leadership, advancement, big adventure and service. We do it all!
  • Providing Community Service - connecting with the community in "duty to others."
  • Using Teamwork - Together Everyone Achieves More.
  • Providing a Safe Environment - zero tolerance for bullying, foul language or crude, inappropriate behavior or conversation.
  • Enabling Advancement - planning to help each scout succeed to his desired level. Being Supportive - inside and outside of Scouting, we celebrate each Scout's success.

Meeting Location

We meet at Garden Street United Methodist Church in Bellingham, Washington, every thursday of every month(minus holidays).
